## Step 1 — Structured extraction

**Finding 1 — GEM (Meta)**
- feature_name: Generative Ads Recommendation Model (GEM)
- problem_solved: one LLM-scale ads foundation model that transfers knowledge to the whole ads model fleet instead of improving models one by one
- ml_technique: Wukong-based stackable factorization machines + InterFormer sequence/cross-feature interleaving, multi-domain learning, post-training knowledge transfer
- claimed_results: +5% ad conversions on Instagram and +3% on Facebook Feed in Q2; 4x architecture efficiency; 2x knowledge-transfer effectiveness vs standard distillation — **claimed by Meta (tier 2 eng blog)**
- evidence_tier: 2

**Finding 2 — REA (Meta)**
- feature_name: Ranking Engineer Agent (REA)
- problem_solved: manual ML experimentation loop (hypothesis → training → debug → iterate) is the bottleneck for ads ranking improvement
- ml_technique: autonomous agent with hibernate-and-wake long-horizon execution, dual-source hypothesis engine (historical insights DB + ML research agent), three-phase planning under compute budgets
- claimed_results: 2x average model accuracy over baseline across six models; 5x engineering output — **claimed by Meta (tier 2 eng blog)**
- evidence_tier: 2

**Finding 3 — HSRL (Kuaishou)**
- feature_name: Hierarchical Semantic RL (HSRL), WWW 2026
- problem_solved: RL for ranking is unstable because the action space (the ad catalog) is huge and dynamic
- ml_technique: encode items as hierarchical semantic-ID token sequences → fixed semantic action space; coarse-to-fine hierarchical policy + multi-level critic for token-level credit assignment
- claimed_results: +18.421% advertiser expected spend in a 7-day online A/B; deployed in Kuaishou's ads ranking module — **claimed by Kuaishou via a tier-4 secondary writeup of a tier-1 WWW 2026 paper** (zh source)
- evidence_tier: 4 (writeup) summarizing tier-1 work; primary paper: arxiv 2510.09167

**Finding 4 — Adaptive Ranking Model (Meta)**
- feature_name: Meta Adaptive Ranking Model
- problem_solved: serving LLM-scale ranking models under sub-second latency and cost limits
- ml_technique: request-oriented computation sharing, Wukong Turbo, selective FP8, multi-card O(1T) parameter serving
- claimed_results: +3% ad conversions, +5% ad CTR on Instagram since Q4 2025 — **claimed by Meta (tier 2 eng blog)**
- evidence_tier: 2

*Also observed, out of this question's scope (bidding, not ranking): Kuaishou LBM — hierarchical LLM auto-bidding (LBM-Think/LBM-Act, GQPO offline RL fine-tuning). Ask for a bidding-focused pass to get its brief.*
